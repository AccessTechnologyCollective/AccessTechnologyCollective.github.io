Description

Join us to learn about iOS accessibility features that can assist users with visual impairments including:

  • Zoom: Allows users to zoom in on the screen to make text and other content larger.
  • Bold Text: Makes text thicker and easier to read.
  • Larger Text: Makes text larger.
  • Invert Colors: Inverts the colors on the screen, making it easier to read text on high-contrast backgrounds.
  • VoiceOver: A screen reader that speaks the contents of the screen, including text, buttons, and other elements.
