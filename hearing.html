Description

In this session we will cover features found in the Hearing section of the Accessibility settings on iOS Apple devices:

  • Live Listen: Allows you to use your iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch as a microphone, sending sound to your headphones, hearing aid or other compatible device.
  • Live Closed Captions: Displays real-time written transcripts of spoken dialogue.
  • Sound Recognition: Alerts you to specific sounds, such as a doorbell or a smoke alarm, using a notification or vibration.
  • Audio Descriptions: Provides spoken descriptions of the visual elements of a video, such as actions, settings, and scene changes.
