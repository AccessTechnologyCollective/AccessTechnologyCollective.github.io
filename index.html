The ATC Presents
Spring 2023 Learning Series - iOS Accessibility
January 25th and 26th

The ATC is excited to be able to offer a series of presentations centered around the amazing accessibility features available on iOS mobile devices including iPhones, iPads and the Apple Watch.

Inspired by Apple's latest "The Greatest" campaign, we will be offering four one-hour sessions covering a range of accessibility topics over two days. Select topic info buttons below for more details and registration links. Join us for the greatest ATC Spring learning series ever!

Sessions open to all faculty, staff and students of Region E.
Flex Credit Available. Accommodation requests or inquiries please contact htcdsps21@gmail.com by January 18th.

Accessibility Overview
Wednesday, Jan. 26 - 12pm

This session will present a general overview of the various accessibility features available on Apple devices. Join us to experience the future of accessibility!

Overview Details

Reading
Wednesday, Jan. 26 - 1pm

Enhance your reading and writing with audio. Hear your entire screen read aloud with Speak Screen. Strip away ads, buttons, and navigation bars, so you can focus on only the content you need.

Reading Details

Hearing
Thursday Jan. 26th - 9am

All about iOS Hearing offerings including the new Live Captions feature for real-time transcriptions, and Sound Recognition which alerts when selected sounds are detected.

Hearing Details

Vision
Thursday Jan. 26th - 10am

All things vision at this workshop! Learn how to make your device work for you. We will cover text size, display settings, dictation, magnification, Voice Over, and more!
